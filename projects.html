Scratch

Scratch is a free Website where you can either create games or play games that other people created. It is very easy to learn because you dont write the code, you built it with blocks. Every single object in the game you create has an own code.

My project is called Pong at it is as the title says classic Pong. You have a paddle that follows your mouse all around the screen. Then there is the ball which moves in one direction and bounces from the edges and the paddle. If you dont hit the ball and it lands under the paddle you loose. To win you have to destroy all the blocks that are placed at the top of the screen. Everythime you destroy a block you get +1 score. When you destroy all 8 of them you win!
